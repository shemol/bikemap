The addresses or names that you enter in the From and To fields are interpreted in the context of the visible area of the map. Results within the box are preferred to results elsewhere.

Using the "Region" field is a quick way to zoom the map to your location.

If you prefer not to let your browser perform automatic geolocation, of course, you can always enter your own text or manually zoom the map to the proper location.

The From selection is the starting address or place that your bike ride will start.

You don't have to enter anything here, if you want your browser to autodetect your current location.

The To selection is your final destination.

You can specify this as a street address ("1234 Main Street") or as a destination ("Betty's Coffee Main St").

We'll use the closest matching place that's visible on the map.
